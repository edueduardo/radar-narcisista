## WHAT IT DOES

Radar Narcisista helps people gain clarity about potentially abusive relationships.

**How it works:**
1. User journals daily episodes and feelings
2. AI detects patterns of manipulation, gaslighting, abuse
3. Dashboard shows risk indicators over time
4. AI Coach helps user reflect and understand
5. Optional: Professional (therapist) can monitor

**Who uses it:**
- People in confusing/toxic relationships
- Psychologists and therapists
- Clinics and NGOs
